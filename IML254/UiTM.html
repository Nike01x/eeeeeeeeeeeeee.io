My experience


UiTM Kedah is a beautiful place albeit it's small in size. The nature here is unbelievable and every classroom has aircond! I'm in Library Informatics IM144 which recently has been in their faculty called information management. If you are a guy, I highly recommend this course because, in my batch, it currently lacks men. you can see in the picture below the ratio between men and women. It's staggering. Overall, it's truly a unique experience getting into university. I'm the first in my family who managed to enter one after all so I also carried my family name coming here. UiTM Kedah also has 2 lakes and one of them even has a cafe right beside it!
